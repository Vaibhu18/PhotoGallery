1st Hundred

Probably the most memorable innings of his IPL career thus far, Virat Kohli scored

2st Hundred

Virat Kohli scored the second century of his Twenty20 (T20) career when he scored an unbeaten 108 off 58 balls
to lead Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) to a sensational 7-wicket win in Match 34 of Indian Premier League
(IPL) 2016 against Rising Pune Supergiants (RPS) at Bangalore.





3st Hundred

Kohli smashed 109 off 55 balls against Gujarat Lions to register his 3rd ton in IPL 2016.
The right-hander, in an incredible display of hitting, blasted 8 sixes and 5 fours.





4st Hundred

Royal challengers Bangalore captain Virat Kohli played an unbelievable innings yesterday against KXIP when he scored
his 4th century of this IPL season. Kohli scored 113 runs in just 50-balls and walked off to a standing ovation.





5st Hundred

On this day in 2019, Royal Challengers Bangalore captain Virat Kohli registered his fifth century in the
Indian Premier League during the 35th match of the 12th season against Kolkata Knight Riders in Kolkata.
